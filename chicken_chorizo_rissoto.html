Chicken and Chorizo Rissoto

Ingredients

180g Risotto Rice
3x Chicken OXO cubes
2 tbsp Red Wine Vinegar
1x Onion
2x Garlic Cloves
500g Passata
2 tbsp Tomato Purée
130g Chorizo
3x Chicken Breasts
Mozzerella (1 or 2 Balls)
4 tbsp Double Cream
1 tbsp Mixed Herbs

Staples:

Salt
Pepper
Water

Method

1) Dice onion and Garlic

2) Heat the oil in a large, non-stick pan on a medium/high heat and add onions until softened

3) Add garlic for 2 mins

4) Dice chicken into small bitesize pieces, add to pan and fry until cooked through

5) Add 600g boiling water to chicken OXO cubes, stirring well to make the stock

6) Once the chicken has cooked add rice, tomato purée and 1 tbsp mixed herbs to the pan. Mix well and cook for 2 mins

7) Add passata and cook for 10 mins stirring continuously

8) Add chicken stock in 3rds, again stirring continuously. Add the next 3rd once most of the liquid has disappeared (whole process should take 40 mins ish). More boiling water can be added if rice isn't soft once all the stock has been used

9) With 10 mins or so left fry chorizo until cooked (chorizo can be cut to your liking if you've bought the sausage version)

10) Add chorizo and the oil to the risotto

11) Add red wine vinegar

12) Just before serving add double cream and stir for 2 minutes

13) Dry off Mozzarella balls and cut into slices (1 or 2 balls depending on how much you like)

14) Serve risotto placing mozzerella slices on top

15) Bon Appétit!

To Serve:

Serve with a side salad and/or garlic bread
