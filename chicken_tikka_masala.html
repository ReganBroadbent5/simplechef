Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients

Chicken Pieces

3x Chicken Breasts
120g Greek Yoghurt
2x Garlic Cloves
2 tbsp Ginger
1/2 Lemon
1 tsp Ground Coriander
1/2 tsp Tumeric
1/2 tsp Cumin
1 tsp Paprika
1/2 tsp Hot Chilli Powder
Pinch of Cinnamon

Masala Sauce

1 tbsp Butter
1x Onion
3 Garlic Gloves
2 tsp Ginger
1 tsp Garam Masala
2 tsp Hot chilli Powder
2 tsp Ground Coriander
2 tsp Paprika
1 tsp Cumin
1/2 tsp cinnamon
2 tbsp tomato purée
1x Red Pepper
400g Chopped Tomatoes
180ml Double Cream
2 tbsp Ground Almonds
1 tbsp Brown Sugar

Staples:

Sunflower Oil
Salt
Pepper
Water

Method

** 2-3 hours minimum before cooking marinate the chicken pieces (steps 1-3). In these stpes use the ingredients and quantities listed under the 'Chicken Pieces' section **

1) Dice chicken into bitesize pieces and put in a large bowl

2) Mince the garlic and ginger

3) Add all the ingredients apart from the yoghurt to the chicken and combine well

4) Add the yoghurt and combine well. Cover in cling film and put in fridge until cooking starts ** The rest of the recipe refers to ingredients under 'Masala Sauce' **

5) Dice onion and pepper as well as mince the garlic and ginger

6) Heat the oil in a large, non-stick pan on a medium/high heat and add onions until softened

7) Combine garam masala, ground coriander, paprika, cumin, chilli powder and cinnamon in a bowl to make a spice mix

8) Add garlic, ginger, spice mix and tomato purée to the onions and cook for 2/3 mins

9) Add in red pepper, tinned tomatoes and 120ml water and bring to boil. Then simmer for about 20 mins until thickened to your likening

10) Spread the pre marinated chicken onto a baking tray (recommend lining with tin foil)

11) Grill until cooked through and slightly blackened on top (can flip halfway if charring too much on top)

12) When the sauce if nearly ready, stir in the cream, ground almonds and brown sugar, followed by the chicken and cook for a further 5 mins

13) Bon Appétit!

To Serve:

Serve with basmati rice and your favourite indian snacks and sides
