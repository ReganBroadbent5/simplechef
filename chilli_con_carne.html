Chilli Con Carne

Ingredients

500g Minced Beef
1x Onion
400g Chopped Tomatoes
400g Kidney Beans in chilli sauce*
2x Garlic cloves
2 tbsp Tomato purée
1/2 tsp Hot Chilli Powder**
2 tsp Mild Chilli Powder**
1 tsp Mixed Herbs
1x Beef Stock

Staples:

Olive Oil
Salt
Pepper
Water

* can use regular kidney beans instead and can use a smaller 200g can if you prefer
** can use any heat of chilli powder to your taste. SimpleChef finds the combination suggested not too hot but with a kick of heat

Method

1) Dice onion and Garlic

2) Heat the oil in a large, non-stick pan on a mediukm/high heat and add onions until softened

3) Add garlic for 2 minutes. Boil the kettle

4) Add the mince, add 1/2 tsp mild chilli powder and a touch of salt and pepper and stir all together until the mince is browned

5) Push mince to the side of the pan and add tomato purée and mixed herbs into the empty side, cooking for 2 minutes

6) Mix everything in the pan together and turn heat down to low

7) Mix in remaining chilli powder (1/2 tsp hot, 1 1/2 tsp mild)

8) Mix in chopped tomatoes

9) Mix in kidney beans

10) Put stock in empty kidney bean can and fill with water so it just covers the stock. Mix well with a fork

11) Mix in stock mixture

12) Turn up the heat to just below medium and simmer until it reaches the consistency you like

13) Bon Appétit!

To Serve:

Recommended is serving with basmati rice, grated cheese, soure cream and tangy cheese doritos
