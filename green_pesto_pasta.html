Green Pesto Pasta

with sundried tomatoes, ham and feta cheese

Ingredients

400g Fusilli Pasta
190g Green Pesto
285g Sundried Tomatoes
200g Feta Cheese
125g Wafer Thin Ham

Staples:

Salt
Pepper
Water

Method

1) Boil the pasta as instructed in heavily salted water

2) Whilst the pasta is cooking, cut the ham, feta and sundried tomatoes in small bitesize pieces

3) Once the pasta is cooked drain1/2 cup of the pasta water into a mixing bowl, before drianign the rest of the water and adding the pasta into the mixing bowl

4) Generously add pepper to the pasta along with the pesto and mix well

5) Add in the remaining chopped up ingredients and mix well

6) Transfer to containers and place in fridge ready to eat

7) Bon Appétit!



SimpleChef developed this recipe to be eaten cold as a work lunch

For best results remove from fridge 2 hours before eating

Ham can be substituted for any cold meat. SimpleChef recommends German Style Peppered Salami as a delicious alternative
