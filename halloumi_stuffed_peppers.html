Halloumi Stuffed Peppers

Ingredients

3x Red Peppers
225g Halloumi
4x Spring Onions
75g Couscous
1x Veg OXO Cube
6x Plum Tomatoes
1 tsp Paprika
1x Garlic Clove
1/2 tsp Mixed Herbs
1 1/2 tsp Red Pesto
1x Red Chilli

Staples:

Olive Oil
Salt
Pepper

Method

1) Pre-heat oven to 190 degrees

2) Cut peppers in half lengthways and remove middle and stalk

3) Place on peppers on baking tray (inside up)

4) Mix garlic with olive oil and brush over each pepper

5) Cover with foil and put in oven for 30 mins

6) Mix halloumi, spring onions, tomatoes, pesto, red chilli, paprika, mixed herbs, salt, pepper, and a bit of olive oil in a bowl

7) Prepare coucous as directed, using the veg stock as the liquid

8) Mix couscous into the halloumi mix

9) Remove peppers from oven after 30 mins and drain excess oil from inside them

10) Stuff each pepper equally with halloumi mix

11) Return the tray uncovered to oven on 170 degrees for 15 mins

12) Remove from oven and serve
