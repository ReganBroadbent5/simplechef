Halloumi Stuffed Peppers

Ingredients

Red peppers 3
Halloumi 1 (250g cut into squares)
Spring onions 4 (cut into small rings)
Couscous 75g
Veg stock 1 cube (however much is needed for couscous)
Plum tomatoes 6 (cut into ¼s)
Paprika 3 shakes
Garlic clove 1 (diced)
Mixed herbs sprinkle
Pesto 1 ½ tsps.
Red chilli 1 (diced)

Staples:

Olive oil
Salt
Pepper

Method

1) Cut peppers in half lengthways and remove middle and stalk

2) Place on peppers on baking tray (inside up)

3) Mix garlic with olive oil and brush over each pepper

4) Cover with foil and put in oven for 30 mins (190)

5) Meanwhile, mix halloumi, spring onions, tomatoes, pesto, red chilli, paprika, mixed herbs, salt, pepper, and a bit of olive oil in a bowl

6) Prepare couscous with the veg stock

7) Mix couscous into the halloumi mix

8) Remove peppers from oven after 30 mins and drain excess oil from inside them

9) Stuff each pepper equally with halloumi mix

10) Return, uncovered, to oven (170) for 15 mins

11) Bon Appétit!
