Halloumi Stuffed Peppers

Ingredients

2x Red Peppers
225g Halloumi
4x Spring Onions
110g Couscous (Recommend Mediterranean)
1x Veg OXO Cube
6x Plum Tomatoes
1 tsp Paprika
1x Garlic Clove
1/2 tsp Mixed Herbs
1 1/2 tsp Red Pesto
1x Red Chilli (or 1/2 tsp Hot Chilli Powder)

Staples:

Olive Oil
Salt
Pepper

Method

1) Pre-heat oven to 190 degrees

2) Cut peppers in half lengthways and remove middle and place on a baking tray with the inside placing up

3) Mix garlic with olive oil and brush over each pepper

4) Cover with foil and put in oven for 30 mins

5) Cut halloumi into small cubes. Also cut the tomatoes, spring onions and red chilli (if using) into small pieces

6) Mix halloumi, spring onions, tomatoes, pesto, chilli, paprika, mixed herbs, salt, pepper, and a bit of olive oil in a bowl

7) Prepare coucous as directed, using the veg stock as the liquid

8) Mix 3/4 of the couscous into the halloumi mix

9) Remove peppers from oven after 30 mins and drain excess oil from inside them

10) Stuff each pepper equally with halloumi mix

11) Return the tray uncovered to oven on 170 degrees for 15 mins

12) Remove from oven, place the stuffed peppers on a bed of the remaining couscous

13) Bon Appétit!

To Serve:

SimpleChef recommends having these peppers alongside a salmon fillet or with potato wedges
