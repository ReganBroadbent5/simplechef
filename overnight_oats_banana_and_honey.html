Overnight Oats

with banana and honey

Ingredients

50g Oats
100ml Milk
100g Greek Yoghurt
1 tbsp Honey
1 tsp Cinnamon
1 Banana

Method

1) Prepare this the night before if having for breakfast or at least 8 hours before eating

2) Add oats, milk, yoghurt, cinnamon and honey to a sealable container and mix well

3) Put in fridge for at least 8 hours

4) Once rested, chop banana into thin slices and add to oats and mix well
