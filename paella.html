Paella

Ingredients

1 x Onion
1x Red Pepper
2x Garlic Cloves
1x Chicken Breast
1 and 1/2 tsp Tumeric
1 and 1/2 tsp Paprika
750ml Chicken Stock
250g Paella rice
150g King Prawns
150g Mussels
130g Chorizo
1 x Lemon

Staples:

Sunflower Oil
Salt
Pepper
Water

Method

1) Dice onion and Garlic

2) Heat the oil in a large, non-stick pan on a medium/high heat and add onions and red pepper until softened

3) Add garlic, tumeric and paprika for 2 mins

4) Dice chicken into small bitesize pieces, add to pan and fry until cooked through

5) Add rice for 2 mins

6) Pour in all stock and bring to boil for 5 mins

7) Turn heat down and simmer for 20 mins stirring occasionally

8) Fry chorizo until cooked (chorizo can be cut to your liking if you've bought the sausage version)

9) Add prawns and mussels to the chorizo mix

10) Squeeze 1/4 lemon into the meat pan and 1/4 into the paella

11) Once most of the stock has absorbed and the rice is soft, add the meat pan contents to paella

12) Stir and cook for a further 5 mins

13) Serve Paella with a lemon wedge each

14) Bon Appétit!
