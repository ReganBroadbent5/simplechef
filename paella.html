Paella

Ingredients

Onion 1 (diced)
Red pepper 1 (diced)
Garlic cloves 2 (diced)
Chicken breast 1 (cubed into smallish pieces)
Turmeric 3 shakes
Paprika 3 shakes
Chicken stock 750ml (3 oxo cubes)
Risotto rice 250g
King prawns 1 pack
Mussels 1 pack
Chorizo Pack of little chunks
Lemon - 1

Staples:

Sunflower oil
Salt
Pepper

Method

1) Fry onion and red pepper for 5 mins in some oil

2) Stir in garlic, turmeric and paprika for 2 mins

3) Add in chicken and cook till it goes white

4) Pour in all stock and bring to boil for 5 mins

5) Add rice and let simmer for 20 mins (cover most of pan with baking tray and stir occasionally)

6) Fry chorizo in a separate pan for a couple of mins

7) Add prawn and mussels to chorizo

8) Cut lemon into 4 and squeeze juice of ¼ into meat pan

9) Once most of stock has absorbed and rice is soft, add chorizo, prawns, and mussels

10) Stir and cook for 5 mins

11) Serve lemon wedges with paella

12) Bon Appétit!
