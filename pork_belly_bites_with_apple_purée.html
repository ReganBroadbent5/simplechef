Pork Belly Bites with Apple Purée

with dauphinoise potatoes and tenderstem broccoli

Ingredients

12x Pork belly strips
2 tsp Paprika
6 x Bramley Apples
70g Butter
2 tbsp Light brown sugar
4 x Potatoes
400ml Full fat milk
400ml Double cream
2x Garlic Cloves
Grated cheese (Medium cheddar)
Tenderstem Broccoli
1 tbsp Balsamic vinegar

Staples:

Olive oil
Salt
Pepper
50ml Water

Method

1) Peel and slice the potatoes into thin slices roughly 4mm thick

2) Heat cream, milk and garlic in a saucepan until starting to simmer

3) Add sliced potatoes to the pan for 15 mins, stirring every few minutes

4) Pre-heat the oven to 200 degrees

5) Whilst the potatoes are in the pan, peel and core the apples and slice in half

6) Add apples into a bowl with the sugar, a pinch of salt and the cubed butter. Toss to mix everything together and transfer to a heatproof dish

7) Rub pork belly strips with salt, pepper and paprika and lie fat side up on a tin foiled baking tray. Drizzle with olive oil

8) Once the 15 mins are up on the potatoes transfer to an ovenproof dish, delicately creating layers of potatoes. Once half way add a layer of grated chese, then continue building layers of potato until all the potato had gone and finally add another layer of cheese to the top

9) Put pork belly strips, apple mixture and dauphinoise potatoes into the oven. Pork and Potatoes for 45 minutes and appled for 25 minutes

10) After 25 minutes remove apple mixture from the oven and transfer contents to a blender, whisk until smooth

11) Pass apple mixture through a fine sieve to further smooth the purée (apple purée now finished, can heat up in microwave for 30 seconds if cold by service)

12) With 10 minutes to go, heat olive oil in a large flat pan on a medium flame

13) Once hot add tenderstem brocolli with a pinch of salt and pepper. Cook for 10 mins, flipping half way through

14) Remove pork belly strips and dauphinoise potatoes from ovenproof

15) Add a drizzle of balsamic vinegar onto the tenderstem broccoli for the last 30 seconds

16) To serve: Place the prok belly strips onto of the apple purée, slice the dauphinoise potatoes into even parts and add the broccoli

17) Bon Appétit!
