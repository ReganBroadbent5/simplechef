Pork Belly Bites with Apple Purée

with dauphinoise potatoes and tenderstem broccoli

Ingredients

12x Pork belly strips
2 tsp Paprika
6x Bramley Apples
70g Butter
2 tbsp Light Brown Sugar
4x Potatoes
400ml Full Fat Milk
400ml Double Cream
2x Garlic Cloves
Grated Cheese (Recommended: Medium cheddar)
Tenderstem Broccoli
1 tbsp Balsamic Vinegar

Staples:

Olive Oil
Salt
Pepper
Water

Method

1) Peel and slice the potatoes into thin slices roughly 4mm thick

2) Heat cream, milk and garlic in a saucepan until starting to simmer

3) Add sliced potatoes to the pan for 15 mins, stirring every few minutes

4) Pre-heat the oven to 200 degrees

5) Whilst the potatoes are in the pan, peel and core the apples and slice in half

6) Add apples into a bowl with the sugar, a pinch of salt and the cubed butter. Toss to mix everything together and transfer to a heatproof dish

7) Rub pork belly strips with salt, pepper and paprika and lie fat side up on a tin foiled baking tray. Drizzle with olive oil

8) Once the 15 mins are up on the potatoes transfer to an ovenproof dish, delicately creating layers of potatoes. Once half way add a layer of grated chese, then continue building layers of potato until all the potato is used

9) Pour over the cream mixture until it just about covers the potatoes and add another layer of cheese to the top

10) Put pork belly strips, apple mixture and dauphinoise potatoes into the oven. Pork and Potatoes for 45 minutes and appled for 25 minutes

11) After 25 minutes remove apple mixture from the oven and transfer contents to a blender, blend until smooth

12) Pass apple mixture through a fine sieve to further smooth the purée (apple purée now finished, can heat up in microwave for 30 seconds if cold by service)

13) Heat olive oil in a large flat pan on a medium flame, once hot add tenderstem brocolli with a pinch of salt and pepper. Cook for 12 mins, flipping half way through

14) Remove pork belly strips and dauphinoise potatoes from ovenproof

15) Add a drizzle of balsamic vinegar onto the tenderstem broccoli for the last 30 seconds

16) To serve: Place the prok belly strips onto of the apple purée, slice the dauphinoise potatoes into even parts and add the broccoli

17) Bon Appétit!
