Spaghetti Bolognaise

Ingredients

1x Onion
2x Garlic Cloves
1 x Red Wine Stock Pot
500g Beef Mince
400g Chopped Tomatoes
2 tbsp Tomato Purée
1 tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
300g Spaghetti
2 tsp Mixed Herbs
Knob of Butter
Parmesan Cheese (Optional)

Staples:

Sunflower Oil
Salt
Pepper
Water

Method

1) Dice onion and garlic

2) Heat the oil in a large, non-stick pan on a medium/high heat and add onions until softened

3) Add garlic for 2 minutes

4) Add the mince to the onion and garlic and stir all together until the mince is browned

5) Push mince to the side of the pan and add tomato purée and mixed herbs into the empty side, cooking for 2 minutes

6) Add chopped tomatoes and stir

7) Put red wine stock pot in empty chopped tomatoes can and add boiling water so it just covers the stock and mix. Pour this into the pan and mix well

8) Bring to boil then simmer for 20-25 mins until getting your preferred consistency

9) With 15 mins left cook the spaghetti in boiling salt water as instructed by the packaging (usually 12 mins)

10) Once the spaghetti is cooked drain and return to pan. Add pepper and a knob of butter and mix through

11) Serve the spaghetti and top with the bolognaise sauce. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese and extra pepper as you like

12) Bon Appétit!

To Serve:

Serve with garlic bread and/or a balsamic side salad
