Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

100g Pancetta
100g Parmesan
3x large eggs
350g Spaghetti
2x Garlic cloves
50g Unsalted butter

Staples:

Sunflower oil
Salt
Pepper
50ml Water

Method

1) Bring pan of water to boil, add Salt

2) Boil spaghetti as per instructions

3) Beat eggs in a bowl and leave aside

4) Peel and squash garlic cloves

5) Fry pancetta and garlic cloves in the butter on medium heat for 5 mins before spaghetti finishes

6) After 5 mins, remove garlic cloves and reduce temp to low

7) When spaghetti ready, use tongs to transfer into pan with pancetta

8) Mix it into the pancetta best you can and add salt and pepper

9) Fry on low heat for 1-2 mins

10) Mix the parmesan thoroughly into the egg mixture

11) Turn off heat on spaghetti and tip the parmesan/egg mixture onto the spaghetti

12) Stir the mixture through the spaghetti, keep it moving to avoid the egg cooking

13) Once mixed through, bring again to low heat, and keep stirring until hot (again don’t let the egg cook)

14) Add salt and pepper and serve

15) Bon Appétit!
