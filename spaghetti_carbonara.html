Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

100g Pancetta
100g Parmesan Cheese
3x Eggs
350g Spaghetti
2x Garlic Cloves
50g Unsalted Butter

Staples:

Sunflower Oil
Salt
Pepper
Water

Method

1) Add salt to a pan of water and bring to boil. Cook Spaghetti as per instructions

2) Beat eggs in a bowl and combine with the parmesan, whisking until fully combined

3) Peel and squash garlic cloves

4) Heat the butter with a tbsp of oil , once hot add pancetta and garlic for 5 mins

5) After 5 mins, remove garlic cloves and reduce temp to low

6) When the spaghetti is ready use tongs to transfer into pan with pancetta and mix, add pepper (you want some pasta water to be transferred during this process)

7) Fry on low heat for 1-2 mins

8) Add 5 tbsp of pasta water to the egg mixture, whisk well

9) Turn off heat on spaghetti and tip the egg mixture onto the spaghetti

10) Stir the mixture through the spaghetti, keep it moving to avoid the egg scrambling

11) Once mixed through, turn to low heat and keep stirring until hot (again do not let the egg scramble)
